A Little Bit About Me
My name's Dylan, and I'm a sophmore at an early college high school currently working toward an associate in computer science. After I graduate high school, I hope to continue studying computer science at the Colorado School of Mines.
I enjoy learning about ways to make front-end applications sleek and appealing as well as programming concepts that can make the back-end of an application secure and efficient. For the spring 2023 semester, I'm taking a python programming course from Arapahoe Community College and a networking course from Aims Community College that will prepare me to earn the CompTIA Networking+ certification.
Software development interests me because the idea of passionately building your own application from the ground up and publishing it to billions of people is inspiring. The fact that writing lines of code on a screen can translate to real-life improvements to people's lives is what fascinates me most about computer science. Working with computers in general has always been a passion of mine, whether it's using them to do graphic design, edit videos, or produce music.
Aside from computer science, my interests include weightlifting, music production, and mixed martial arts. Who knows, maybe we'll find a way to better incorporate computer science with some of these fields in the future!
